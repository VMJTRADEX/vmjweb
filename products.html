Fresh Moringa
Moringa oleifera is indigenous to the sub-Himalayan regions of northern India and Pakistan. The plant is now distributed across tropical zones in Africa, Asia, islands in the Pacific and the Caribbean, and South America. The tree is cultivated and has become naturalised in the wild in many areas of the world.
Moringa Powder
As is evident from its name, moringa powder is made from the freshly harvested leaves of the moringa oleifera tree. Fresh moringa leaf powder is deep green in color and has a rich nutty smell. This powder is prepared by drying the moringa leaves at low temperature such that the plant tissue reaches a fine powdered consistency.
Moringa Seeds
The bark, sap, roots, leaves, seeds, oil and flowers of moringa are used in traditional medicine in several countries. The seeds of moringa are used to obtain oil, which can be used to cook a variety of dishes.
